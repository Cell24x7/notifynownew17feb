# NotifyNow User Guide 🚀

Welcome to NotifyNow! This guide will help you navigate and master our RCS & SMS communication platform.

---

## 1. Getting Started
To begin using NotifyNow, you need an active account and credit balance.
1. **Login**: Use your credentials to access the dashboard.
2. **Setup Wallet**: Ensure you have enough credits to start a campaign.

## 2. Managing RCS Bots
RCS Bots are your business's identity on the messages.
1. **Create Bot**: Go to the "Bots" section and click "Create New Bot".
2. **Details**: Provide Brand Name, Industry, and Website.
3. **Media**: Upload a square logo (224x224px) and a horizontal banner (1440x448px).
4. **Verification**: Submit for verification. Once approved, the system automatically configures the technical settings.

## 3. Creating Message Templates
Before sending a campaign, you need an approved template.
1. **RCS Templates**:
   - **Text Only**: Simple text messages.
   - **Rich Card**: Text with an image and buttons.
   - **Carousel**: Multiple cards that users can swipe.
2. **Variables**: Use `[name]` or `{{name}}` in your template body to personalize messages.

## 4. Launching Campaigns
1. **New Campaign**: Select the channel (RCS/SMS) and your template.
2. **Upload Audience**: Upload a CSV file. The system will automatically detect the "mobile" column.
3. **Variable Mapping**: Map your CSV columns (like Name) to your template placeholders.
4. **Review & Send**: Check the "Estimated Cost" against your "Wallet Balance". If everything is correct, hit "Send Now".

## 5. Wallet & Credits
- Every message has a cost depending on the channel and template type.
- **Auto-Check**: The system will prevent a campaign from starting if your balance is too low.
- **Recharge**: Contact the administrator or your reseller to top up your credits.

---

> [!TIP]
> Always test your campaign with a "Test Send" to your own number before sending it to thousands of customers!
