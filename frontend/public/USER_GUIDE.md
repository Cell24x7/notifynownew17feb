# User Guide - NotifyNow Panel

Welcome to the NotifyNow User Guide. This guide will help you navigate and use the platform effectively.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Login**: Use your registered email and password at [notifynow.in](https://notifynow.in).
- **Dashboard**: Your home screen showing quick stats and recent activity.

## 2. Managing Campaigns
- **Create Campaign**: Go to the "Campaigns" section and click "Create New". Choose between SMS or RCS.
- **Upload Contacts**: You can upload a CSV file with contact numbers or select from your "Contacts" group.
- **Templates**: Select a predefined template or create a new one in the "Templates" section.

## 3. Viewing Reports
- **Real-time Stats**: Go to "Reports" to see delivery, read, and failed statuses in real-time.
- **Download Logs**: You can export your campaign logs for offline analysis.

## 4. Wallet & Billing
- **Check Balance**: Your current balance is always visible in the top bar and in the "Wallet" section.
- **Recharge**: Click on "Recharge" to add funds to your account via the available payment methods.

## 5. Support
If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at **support@notifynow.in**.
