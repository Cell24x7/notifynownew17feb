# NotifyNow Project - Professional Demo Video Script (Hinglish)

Yeh script aapke poore project (Frontend & Backend) ka detailed demo cover karti hai. Isme har ek scene ke liye Screen Actions aur AI Voice lines di gayi hain.

---

### **Scene 1: Welcome & Authentication (Sign Up / Login)**
- **🎯 Focus:** Security aur Entry point.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - Browser mein `http://localhost:3000` (ya live domain) open karein.
  - Landing page se "Get Started" ya direct Auth page par jayein.
  - Sign Up form mein Name, Email, Password bharkar create account karein (Auth validation dikhayien).
  - Phir Sign In karein. Login button click karte hi Dashboard load hote huye dikhayein.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "Namaste! NotifyNow mein aapka swagat hai. Yeh ek ultimate WhatsApp aur RCS messaging platform hai jo aapki reach ko badhane mein madad karta hai. Shuruat karte hain apne account ko securely Sign In karke. Jaise hi hum login karte hain, humare samne aata hai humara powerful, central dashboard."

---

### **Scene 2: Central Dashboard & Analytics**
- **🎯 Focus:** Real-time metrics.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - Dashboard par graphs aur charts scroll karein.
  - Total Sent, Read, Delivered aur Failed messages ke numbers ko highlight karein.
  - Wallet balance box ko mouse se point karein.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "Yeh hai aapka mission control dashboard. Yahan se aap apne saare campaigns ko real-time mein track kar sakte hain. Aap dekh sakte hain kitne messages bheje gaye, kitne read huye aur aapka current wallet balance kya hai. Sab kuch ek hi jagah par, bilkul transparent."

---

### **Scene 3: Contacts Management (Audience Builder)**
- **🎯 Focus:** Database management.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - Side menu se 'Contacts' par click karein.
  - 'Add Contact' button dabayein aur ek demo number (Name, Phone, Tag) add karein.
  - Phir 'Import' button par hover karein aur batayein ki CSV file se bulk import kaise hota hai.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "Marketing ke liye audience zaroori hai. Contacts section mein aap apne customers ka database manage kar sakte hain. Aap manually ek-ek contact add kar sakte hain ya fir hazaron contacts ko ek single CSV file ke zariye bulk import kar sakte hain. Grouping aur tagging ke sath, sahi audience tak pahonchna behad aasan hai."

---

### **Scene 4: Rich Templates (WhatsApp & RCS)**
- **🎯 Focus:** Message creativity.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - 'Templates' page par jayein.
  - 'Create Template' click karein. Form mein Name, Category (Marketing/Utility), aur Language chunein.
  - Body mein message likhein: *"Hi {{1}}, aapke liye 50% discount coupon hai!"*
  - Niche 'Image' select karein aur phir interactive buttons (Call to Action/Quick Reply) add karein.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "Interesting messages hi conversion dilate hain. Templates section mein aap WhatsApp aur RCS dono ke liye visual aur interactive templates bana sakte hain. Body mein dynamic variables use karein taaki har message personal lage. Aap images, videos aur 'Buy Now' jaise interactve buttons bhi jod sakte hain."

---

### **Scene 5: Running a Campaign (Go Live)**
- **🎯 Focus:** Operational flow.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - Sidebar se 'Campaigns' par jayein.
  - 'Create Campaign' flow shuru karein.
  - Campaign name likhein, Provider select karein (Vendor 1/2), aur approve-shuda template pick karein.
  - Contact List select karein aur 'Schedule' ya 'Send Now' par click karein.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "Ab chaliye launch karte hain apna campaign. 'Create Campaign' par click karein, apne campaign ka naam dein, aur apna manpasand message template chunein. Target audience select karne ke baad, aap isse abhi bhej sakte hain ya future ke liye schedule kar sakte hain. Ek baar 'Submit' karne par system sab sambhaal lega."

---

### **Scene 6: Automations & Chatflows (The Brain)**
- **🎯 Focus:** Effortless replies.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - 'Automations/Chatflows' builder open karein.
  - Drag-and-drop nodes dikhayein.
  - Ek 'Trigger' (Keyword like "Offer") se connection line khinch kar 'Send Message' node tak jodein.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "NotifyNow ka asli power hai iska Automation aur Chatflow builder. Bina kisi code ke, aap visual drag-and-drop builder se complex workflows bana sakte hain. Chaho 'auto-reply' set karna ho ya pura customer journey, hamara system 24/7 aapki behalf par kaam karega."

---

### **Scene 7: Live Chats & Inbox**
- **🎯 Focus:** Customer Support.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - 'Chats' page open karein. Left side mein active chats list dikhayein.
  - Ek chat select karein, message history dikhayein aur reply type karke bhejien.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "Human support ke liye humare paas hai unified 'Live Chat' inbox. Yahin se aap har customer ke sath real-time baatcheet kar sakte hain, unki queries solve kar sakte hain aur personalized help de sakte hain."

---

### **Scene 8: Detailed Reports & Analytics**
- **🎯 Focus:** ROI & Data.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - 'Reports' page dikhayein.
  - Delivery rates aur detailed log table (Status, Time, Number) scroll karein.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "Marketing kitni safal rahi, yeh Reports section batata hai. Detailed analysis ke sath aap dekh sakte hain ki message deliver hua, read hua ya fir fail. Data-driven faisle lein aur campaign performance ko improve karein."

---

### **Scene 9: Integrations, Wallet & Logout**
- **🎯 Focus:** Management & Scalability.
- **🎬 Screen Par Kya Dikhana Hai:**
  - Tezi se 'Integrations' (API/Webhooks) dikhayein.
  - 'Wallet' ya 'User Plans' par click karke recharge options dikhayein.
  - Aakhir mein Top Right icon par click karke 'Logout' karein.
- **🎙️ AI Voice (Hinglish):**
  "NotifyNow lachili (flexible) hai. 'Integrations' ke zariye aap apni APIs connect kar sakte hain aur 'Wallet' se apna balance manage kar sakte hain. Har feature ko user-centric banaya gaya hai. Aaj hi join karein aur apne business message experience ko badal dein. Dhanyawad!"
